WORK ENVIRONMENT

The work environment at PL is quite friendly and open. We take a collaborative approach to management. Sharing of ideas is encouraged and cross functional teams towards projects are a common occurrence.

Our meritocratic approach ensure through a robust appraisal system that the top performers are suitably rewarded and others motivated through regular feedback sessions to realise their potential.

It’s not all work and no fun at PL!

We usually celebrate popular holidays and festivals like Diwali, Holi, Chirstmas, Sankranti (Kite festival), Women’s Day etc. We encourage employees to participate in various CSR activities for helping reputed charitable organisations like Childline Foundation, Light of Life Trust and PL Charitable Trust.
