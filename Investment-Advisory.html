Derivatives Segment

The Trader’s Edge bouquet offers a variety of positional trading and market-neutral strategies deploying derivatives trading. These strategies are developed using proprietary algorithms and continuously optimised to give you quick advice on stocks and indices. Each positional trade comes with a stop loss, a trailing stop loss and uses only the most liquid and fundamentally-sound instruments. In addition, a portfolio-level stop loss is also advised to ensure that a deep drawdown doesn’t impact a portfolio beyond a point. Trader’s Edge participates in both, long and short sides – using all potential volatility to your advantage. So if you are a positional trader, it is quite likely this what you are looking for.

  • Trader’s Edge (Indices) : Positional Trades in Nifty/Bank Nifty with an average duration of 1 to 5 days typically
  • Trader’s Edge (Pairs) : A market-neutral Long/Short strategy that looks at profiting from breakdowns in relationships between two highly-correlated stocks and mean reversion

Highlights

  • Thoroughly back-tested strategies using our proprietary algorithmic platforms
  • Disciplined implementation of stop loss on trades that go wrong
  • Trades highly-liquid instruments to minimise slippages
  • Participates in both, long and short sides
  • Instantaneous trade alerts
  • Recommendations move to Pause mode during index/security-impacting events, such as government or RBI policy announcements, and results

Minimum investment advised

  • Rs 15 Lakh for Pairs
  • Rs 2 lakh for any single index/Rs 4 lakh for both indices
  • Minimum 25% of this margin in cash and the rest in NSE-approved FNO stocks
