IPO

Introduction to IPO :

An Initial Public Offering (IPO) is an opportunity for a company as well as investors. The company raises capital by selling its shares, becoming more regulated and transparent. Investors benefit by availing an opportunity to invest at an early stage.

Why should you invest in IPOs :

  • By investing at an early stage you get the opportunity to avail stakes in a promising company. Stakes that would later reap rich dividends
  • IPO investments are also good for long term investors By investing in an IPO, you also align with the company’s long term vision for success and growth
  • IPOs are transparent in terms of pricing The conditions for entry are the same, regardless of whether you’re an institutional investor or retail investor

Things to keep in mind before investing in IPOs :

  • Determine your essential investment criteria. This includes the money that you can invest, risk appetite and time horizon for investment
  • Study offerings carefully before investing Make sure you research the company’s fundamentals, valuation and historic performance before investing
