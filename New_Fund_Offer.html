New Fund Offer

A new fund offer (NFO) is the first subscription offering for any new fund offered by an investment company. A new fund offer occurs when a fund is launched, allowing the firm to raise capital for purchasing securities.

Mutual funds are one of the most common new fund offerings marketed by an investment company. The initial purchasing offer for a new fund varies by the fund’s structuring.A new fund offering is similar to an initial public offering.

Common Fund Offerings

Open-ended and Close-ended Funds Open-ended funds do not have a limit on the number of shares they are issuing whereas Close-ended funds issue a fixed number of shares during their New Fund Offer.
