4 Reasons to Buy Home Insurance

  • It takes years to build your dream house, so protect it from natural disasters like Fire, Earthquake, Cyclone and Flood.
  • 2
  • Protect your dream home from man-made risk like Burglary, Riots, Theft and Terrorism.
  • 3
  • Cover your valuables items like furniture, artifacts, electronic appliances and more.
  • 4
  • You don’t need to renew home insurance policy every year as you can buy a long term cover like 5 years or 10 years.
